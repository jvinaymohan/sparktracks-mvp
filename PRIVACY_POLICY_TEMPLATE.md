Welcome to Sparktracks MVP ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

When you register for an account, we collect:

### Usage Data
We automatically collect:

We use collected information for:

1. **Account Management**
   - Creating and maintaining your account
   - Authenticating users
   - Managing user profiles

2. **Service Functionality**
   - Facilitating task management
   - Enabling calendar and scheduling features
   - Processing points and rewards
   - Sending notifications about tasks and activities

3. **Improvement and Analytics**
   - Analyzing app usage patterns
   - Improving user experience
   - Developing new features
   - Troubleshooting technical issues

4. **Communication**
   - Sending important updates about the service
   - Responding to user inquiries
   - Sending notifications (with your permission)

5. **Safety and Security**
   - Protecting against fraud and abuse
   - Ensuring platform security
   - Complying with legal obligations

## How We Share Your Information

### We Do NOT Sell Your Data

   - Data stored in secure Firebase servers

2. **Service Providers**
   - Third-party services that help operate our app
   - Only for specific tasks and under strict confidentiality

3. **Legal Requirements**
   - If required by law or legal process

4. **With Your Consent**
   - When you explicitly authorize us to share specific information

### Within App Users
- Parents can view information about their registered children
- Coaches can view information about assigned students

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption**: Data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS
- **Firebase Security**: Data stored in Firebase Firestore with security rules
- **Authentication**: Secure user authentication through Firebase Auth
- **Access Control**: Role-based access to ensure users only see authorized data
- **Regular Updates**: We regularly update security measures

**However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.**

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: We retain your information while your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
- **Legal Requirements**: Some data may be retained longer if required by law
- **Backups**: Backup copies may persist for up to 90 days
